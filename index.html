Advancing perinatal mental health through the lens of reproductive justice.

We are a community of psychologists and mental health professionals working to improve care, policy, and advocacy for perinatal mental health — grounded in reproductive justice.

What We Do

Advocacy

We advocate for policies that expand access to perinatal mental health care, especially for communities historically excluded from reproductive healthcare.

Education

We offer training and resources to help clinicians integrate reproductive justice principles into perinatal mental health care.

Community

We connect psychologists and allied professionals working at the intersection of maternal mental health and reproductive justice.

Get Involved

Whether you're a psychologist, trainee, or allied professional, there are many ways to get involved with our work. Visit our events page for upcoming opportunities, or reach out to learn more.
